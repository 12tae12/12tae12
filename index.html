About 1t2.pages.dev

1t2.pages.dev is a website that showcases various projects developed by 12Tae12Software.

Project List
  • Cardio: A greeting card project currently being ported from "cardio.webador.com" to 1t2.pages.dev/cardio/index.html.
  • R-A-G: A project dedicated to providing information about rocks and gems, accessible at 1t2.pages.dev/rag.
  • 12Tae12 Productions: A page featuring short films aiming to entertain viewers, located at 1t2.pages.dev/stuff/films/index.
  • WritePad: Text Wizard (WPTW), a simple text editor for devices with limited installation capabilities, accessible at 1t2.pages.dev/wptw.
